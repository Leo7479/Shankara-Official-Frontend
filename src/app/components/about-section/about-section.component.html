Our Story

Where Ancient Tradition Finds Living Voice

❧

Shankara is an Indian classical music ensemble established in 2025 in Kolkata, a city celebrated for its vibrant artistic heritage and enduring love for classical music. Founded with the vision of preserving and promoting the rich traditions of Indian classical music, Shankara brings together dedicated musicians and performers committed to artistic excellence, cultural authenticity, and meaningful musical expression.

Rooted in the timeless traditions of ragas and talas, Shankara's performances are designed to offer audiences an immersive journey through melody, rhythm, and emotion. The ensemble combines technical mastery with creative improvisation, creating performances that honor the depth and beauty of Indian classical music while remaining accessible to contemporary listeners. Inspired by the belief that music transcends boundaries and unites people, Shankara strives to create experiences that resonate with audiences of all ages and backgrounds.

Since its inception, the ensemble has performed at various cultural programs, music festivals, and artistic gatherings across India. Notable performances in Kolkata, Guwahati, Odisha and other cities have helped establish Shankara as a promising presence in the Indian classical music community. Through each performance, the group seeks to celebrate India's rich musical heritage while fostering a deeper appreciation for classical traditions.

As it continues to grow, Shankara remains committed to preserving tradition, embracing artistic innovation, and delivering soulful performances that leave a lasting impression. Through passion, discipline, and devotion to the art, Shankara carries forward the timeless spirit of Indian classical music for audiences across the nation.
